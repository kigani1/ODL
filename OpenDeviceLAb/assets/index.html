About the project

  • About the project

    It’s been a long time since the web only lived in our PCs. But thanks to the whole universe of smartphones, tablets and wearables, the web now follows us anywhere and everywhere. Making Waves has created a place to give developers the freedom to explore that universe. We’ve donated a number of smartphones and tablets to the ODL – and the collection will grow over time. Visit our offices in Cracow to see the broad range of mobile devices available to all. Test your work to ensure it meets your expectations. Then you and your clients can be proud of how well your work presents itself across various platforms. And remember, this service is completely free.

  • Visit the lab

    The Lab can be found at Making Waves’ offices, which are located in Cracow’s city center. You can work in our ODL during our working hours Monday-Friday. However, we would ask that you contact us in advance so that we can make sure that the lab will be free for you to use.

    View location »

  • Participate in the project

    The Open Device Lab was created for the community, but can also be created by the community. If you like the idea and would like to contribute, feel free to contact us. We’ll figure out together what’s the best way for you to help out. You can lend or donate a device to the Lab or help us spread the good word.

Visit the lab

The Open Device Lab is located at Making Waves’ Cracow offices. You are welcome to visit us anytime during our working hours: Monday through Friday 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. If that does not suit you, let us know and we’ll try to figure something out.
The lab is popular among our developers as well, so be sure to notify us in advance of your visit so that we can book the ODL just for you. Please review the device library (above) to ensure the devices you want to work with are present in the Lab.
